Common Rules


  • Registration for Legacy’23 should be done only through the forms provided by Mepco Schlenk Engineering College, Sivakasi.

  • The entire registration procedure should be completed on or before 09-09-2023.

  • Maximum number of participants per college is 50 (Fifty) only.

  • Participants should bring bonafide certificate and their college ID card.

  • Maximum of two faculty per college should accompany the participants.

Registration Steps



  1. Download The Excel sheet -> Registration Excel

  2. Fill the excel sheet with a proper details to particpate in events

  3. Fill the G-form by uploading the Excel sheet -> Legacy’23 Registration G-Form

  4. Students Will receive the mail after Successfull Registration
